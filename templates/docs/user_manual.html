1. Getting Started

1.1 What Is FlockIQ?

FlockIQ is an AI-powered poultry farm management platform built for Nigerian and West African farmers. It replaces paper logbooks with a structured digital system that tracks mortality, feed consumption, egg production, water usage, vaccinations, and farm finances — then turns that data into clear, actionable insights.

Every calculation uses real breed standards published by Cobb, Ross, Hy-Line, and ISA — so when FlockIQ tells you your FCR is poor, it is comparing your performance against the same benchmarks used by commercial integrators worldwide.

⚠️ Did not receive the email? Check your spam or junk folder. If the link has expired, log in and click Resend Verification Email from the banner displayed on your dashboard.

1.6 Completing Onboarding (3 Steps)

After email verification, a setup wizard guides you through three steps that must be completed before the dashboard activates.

Step 1 — Create Your Farm

Enter your farm's name, location (address), and state. A farm is your physical property. You can add multiple farms under one account.

Step 2 — Add a House (Pen)

A house is a structure on your farm where birds are kept. Enter the house name and capacity (maximum birds it can hold). One farm can have many houses.

Step 3 — Start Your First Batch

A batch is one production cycle inside a house. Select:

  • Bird type — Broiler or Layer
  • Breed — Cobb 500, Ross 308, Hy-Line Brown, ISA Brown, or a custom name
  • Placement date — when day-old chicks (DOCs) were placed
  • Initial count — number of DOCs placed
  • Hatchery (optional) — where DOCs were sourced (links to the Hatchery Directory)

2. Daily Farm Operations

Why daily logging matters: Feed represents approximately 70% of total poultry production costs. Consistent daily records are the foundation of your Farm Credit Score and the data that powers every AI insight. Missing a week of logs is not just an operational gap — it reduces your credit score and delays anomaly detection.

2.1 Logging Mortality

Record the number of birds that died on a given day. Navigate to Batches → [Batch Name] → Mortality Log → Record Mortality.

FieldDescription
DateDate the deaths occurred (defaults to today)
CountNumber of birds that died
CauseNewcastle Disease, Coccidiosis, Heat Stress, Injury, Unknown (optional)
NotesFree-text observations (optional)

What happens after you save:

  • The batch's live bird count is reduced automatically.
  • The system compares today's mortality against your rolling 30-day average using a Z-score test.
  • If the count is statistically unusual, an anomaly alert is sent via SMS and in-app notification to your farm manager.
  • A financial write-down is posted to the farm ledger for accounting.
⚠️ Validation: You cannot log more deaths than the current live bird count. The form will reject the entry with an error message.

2.2 Logging Feed Consumption

Navigate to Batches → [Batch Name] → Feed Log → Record Feed.

FieldDescription

BrandTopFeeds, Chikun, Ultima, Animal Care, Hybrid, Other (optional)

The system accumulates feed data and uses it to calculate your Feed Conversion Ratio (FCR) — the kg of feed required per kg of live weight gained.

BreedTarget FCRRating at Target

2.3 Logging Egg Production (Layers)

Navigate to Batches → [Batch Name] → Production → Log Eggs.

FieldDescription

The system calculates Hen-Day Percentage (HDP) — the proportion of live hens that laid an egg today. For Hy-Line Brown, the target is ≥ 85%.

2.4 Logging Water Consumption

Navigate to Batches → [Batch Name] → Water Log → Record Water.

FieldDescription

Volume (litres)Total litres consumed that day

Heat adjustment: Every degree above 25°C adds 10% to the expected water requirement. During hot weather, ensure drinkers are always full — water stress reduces both growth rates and egg production within hours.

2.6 Managing Daily Tasks

FlockIQ generates daily tasks automatically at midnight based on your active batches. These include daily mortality count, feed weighing, egg collection, water log, weekly weight sampling (broilers), and vaccination reminders.

To create a custom task: go to Tasks → New Task, enter the title, assign it to a team member, and set a due date. The assigned person receives an in-app notification.

⚠️ Incomplete tasks are reported daily. At 18:00 WAT, an automated report of all incomplete tasks is sent to the farm manager. Consistently incomplete tasks reduce your Operational Consistency sub-score.
✅ Consistent daily logging improves your Farm Credit Score. The Operational Consistency component (20% of your score) measures how regularly you log data across each batch's lifetime. Even logging zero mortality is valuable — it proves you checked and the flock is healthy.

3. AI Insights

3.1 AI Daily Brief

The AI Daily Brief appears at the top of your dashboard each morning. It is generated overnight (between 01:00 and 06:30 WAT) and summarises the most important signals across all your active batches.

Brief SectionWhat It Shows

Egg Production ForecastPredicted hen-day percentage for the next 14 days (layer batches only)
Feed EfficiencyCurrent FCR compared to your breed benchmark and trend direction
Water RequirementToday's recommended water target in litres based on current temperature
Upcoming VaccinationsAny doses due in the next 7 days across all batches
⚠️ Data requirements: The egg forecast requires at least 21 days of egg production logs. Anomaly detection requires at least 7 days of mortality logs. The brief will appear partially until these thresholds are met.

3.2 Farm Memory

Farm Memory is FlockIQ's learning system. Every night at 01:00 WAT, the platform recomputes performance baselines from your closed batches:

  • Average daily mortality by batch age and season
  • Typical FCR trajectory by breed
  • Normal egg production curve for your flock type and local conditions

These baselines make anomaly detection more accurate over time. After 3 closed batches, FlockIQ alerts you to deviations specific to your farm — not generic breed averages. After 6 batches, your Farm Credit Score reaches Established confidence level.

4. Farm Credit Score

4.1 What Is the Farm Credit Score?

The Farm Credit Score is a 0–100 numerical rating of your farm's operational and financial performance across all closed batches. It is designed to serve as a credibility document when applying for farm input loans, feed credit, or agricultural grant programmes.

The score is computed nightly at 03:00 WAT using data from your most recent 12 closed batches. It is accompanied by a letter grade (A+ to F) and a confidence level that tells a lender how much history backs the score.

5. Market Intelligence

5.1 Feed Price Tracker

The Feed Price Tracker is a crowdsourced database of feed prices submitted by FlockIQ users across Nigeria. Prices are aggregated anonymously — your individual submission is never shown to other users. Only regional averages and trends are displayed.

How to Submit a Price Report

  1. Navigate to Market → Feed Prices.
  2. Click Report a Price.
  3. Select the feed type (Broiler Starter, Broiler Grower, Broiler Finisher, Layer Mash, or Layer Chick Mash).
  4. Select the brand (TopFeeds, Chikun, Ultima, Animal Care, Hybrid, or Other).
  5. Enter the price per 25 kg bag in Naira.
  6. Select your state and LGA (Local Government Area).
  7. Click Submit.

Reading the Price Intelligence Dashboard

The price chart shows the community average price per 25 kg bag for each feed type over the past 30 days. Filter by feed type, brand, and state/region. A trend line shows whether prices are rising, falling, or stable in your area.

Why anonymity matters: No individual farm's prices are ever attributed. This encourages honest reporting and prevents market distortion. Your submission contributes to a fair price index that benefits every farmer who uses the platform.

6.3 Closing a Batch

Navigate to Batches → [Batch Name] → Close Batch.

For Broiler Batches

  • Final bird count — live birds at point of harvest
  • Total live weight (kg) — used for final FCR calculation
  • Sale price per kg (₦) — used for revenue and profit calculation
  • Harvest date

For Layer Batches

  • Depletion count — hens sold or culled at batch end
  • Spent layer price per bird (₦)
  • Closing date

After closing, the system generates:

  • A final Batch Performance Report (FCR, mortality rate, hen-day average for layers)
  • A Profit & Loss Summary for the batch
  • An updated Farm Credit Score (recomputed at 03:00 the following night)
  • A hatchery rating prompt (if the batch was linked to a hatchery)

7.2 What Happens When Your Trial Expires

Your 14-day trial provides full access to all features. A countdown banner appears at the top of every page during the final 7 days. Reminder notifications are sent at 7, 3, and 1 day before expiry.

When the trial ends your account enters read-only mode:

  • All your existing data remains visible and accessible
  • New data entry (mortality, feed, eggs, water) is blocked
  • A "Subscribe Now" banner appears on the dashboard
  • No data is ever deleted due to trial expiry

7.3 Renewing Your Plan

  1. Navigate to Settings → Billing (or click the subscription banner).
  2. Select your preferred plan — Monthly, Cycle, or Yearly.
  3. Click Subscribe — you will be redirected to the Paystack checkout.
  4. Enter your card details or complete via bank transfer or USSD.
  5. Your account is activated immediately upon successful payment.

You will receive an email receipt from Paystack and an in-app notification confirming activation.

7.4 What Lapsed Access Means

If you are on a paid plan and the plan expires without renewal, your account enters lapsed status:

  • All existing data remains fully visible (read-only)
  • New data entry is blocked until you renew
  • A Renew Plan button appears on your dashboard
  • Your Farm Credit Score is preserved and visible to you
✅ Your data is always safe. FlockIQ never deletes farm data regardless of subscription status. Even a lapsed or cancelled account retains full data visibility indefinitely.

8.3 Custom Domain Setup

On eligible plans, you can serve FlockIQ from your own domain (e.g., app.myfarm.com) instead of myfarm.flockiq.com. Navigate to Settings → Custom Domain.

  1. Enter your domain in the Custom Domain field (e.g., app.myfarm.com).
  2. Click Generate Verification Token.
  3. Log in to your domain registrar and add a TXT record with the token value shown.
  4. Return to FlockIQ and click Verify Domain. DNS propagation may take up to 24 hours.
  5. Once verified, add a CNAME record pointing to app.flockiq.com.
⚠️ Important order: Complete Step 4 (verification) before Step 5 (CNAME). Pointing your CNAME before verification is confirmed will prevent the domain check from succeeding.

8.4 Exporting Your Data

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), you may request a full export of all data FlockIQ holds about you and your farm. Navigate to Settings → Data & Privacy → Export My Data.

The export ZIP file contains CSV and JSON files covering: all batch records, mortality logs, feed logs, egg logs, water logs, financial records, notification history, and account details.

8.5 Deleting Your Account

Navigate to Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete Account.

⚠️ Deletion is permanent. All data will be erased after a 30-day cooling-off period. You can cancel the deletion by logging in and clicking Cancel Deletion within those 30 days. After the cooling-off period, erasure is irreversible.
