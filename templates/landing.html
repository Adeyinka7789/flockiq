Platform Features

Core Operational Wisdom

Data is noise. Intelligence is signal. We provide the signal required to scale commercial poultry operations.

AI-Powered Flock Insights
Prophet-driven egg-yield forecasting and mortality prediction surface trouble before it hits the flock — so you act early, not after the loss.
Multi-Farm Management
Run every farm and poultry house from one dashboard. Switch sites, compare performance, and roll up consolidated metrics across locations.
Feed Efficiency Tracking
Track feed conversion ratio (FCR) and feed cost per kg of bird or per crate of eggs. Spot waste and tighten margins batch by batch.
Health & Vaccination Records
Full health history per batch — auto-generated vaccination schedules, treatment logs, and drug-withdrawal tracking with timely reminders.
Harvest Timing Optimizer
AI weighs weight curves, feed cost, and market prices to recommend the optimal sell date — so you cash out at peak profit, not guesswork.
Weather-Aware Alerts
Proactive heat-stress, rain, and humidity alerts by farm GPS location — get ahead of local weather before it stresses your birds.
Financial Analytics
Revenue, cost, and profit broken down per batch and per farm. Full P&L, break-even analysis, and spending trends at a glance.
Multi-Tenant & Team Access
Role-based access per organisation. Invite your manager, vet, and farm hands with the right permissions — every action fully audit-trailed.
