Platform Features

Core Operational Wisdom

Data is noise. Intelligence is signal. We provide the signal required to scale commercial poultry operations.

Daily Production Tracking
Log eggs, feed, water, and waste daily. Auto-calculates hen-day %, FCR, and feed requirements by bird age and count.
Health & Vaccination Engine
Auto-generates breed-specific vaccination schedules. SMS reminders 3 days before due date. Drug withdrawal period tracker.
AI Anomaly Detection
Z-score analysis flags abnormal mortality and water drops before they become outbreaks. Real-time SMS alerts to your phone.
Theft Detection
Automatic stock reconciliation flags unexplained bird losses above 1.5% variance. Full audit trail for every movement.
Financial Intelligence
Full P&L per batch. Break-even analysis. Optimal broiler sale timing. Market price tracker and minimum viable price calculator.
Weather Intelligence
Heat stress, rain, and humidity alerts by farm GPS. Seasonal demand forecasting for eggs and live birds. Plan ahead.

About FlockIQ

Built by farmers' tech —
for farmers

FlockIQ is built by ADM Tech Hub, a Lagos-based software engineering agency. We built FlockIQ after seeing Nigerian poultry farmers lose millions every year to problems that technology could prevent.

We are not a foreign company adapting a generic product. FlockIQ is designed from scratch for Nigerian farming realities — intermittent power, mobile-first farmers, Naira pricing, local disease patterns, and local markets.
