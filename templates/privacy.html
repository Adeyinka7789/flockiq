🔒 Privacy Framework v2.4

Your Flock Data,
Precisely Protected.

At FlockIQ, we view data privacy as a fundamental pillar of modern poultry farming. Our systems are designed to ensure your operational insights remain exclusively yours.

The Multi-Tenant Vault

Our architecture employs physical and logical data isolation. Your farm's mortality records, egg production, feed usage, and health alerts are never commingled with other tenants. Each account operates within its own encrypted container.

🔒 Isolated Storage 🚫 Zero-Leak Policy 🔐 AES-256 Encryption
🛡️
✅

NDPR Compliant

Full adherence to Nigeria Data Protection Regulation and international standards for West African poultry operations.

01.

Data Collection

We collect data necessary to provide high-precision poultry intelligence. This is categorized into the following streams:

  • ✓
    Production Telemetry Daily mortality, egg counts, feed consumption, and water usage logged via our mobile-first platform.
  • ✓
    Identity Metadata Account information including farm name, regional location (Lagos, Ibadan, Kano, Abuja), and contact details for system alerts.
  • ✓
    Health & Vaccination Records Breed-specific vaccination schedules, drug withdrawal periods, and disease outbreak history.
02.

Storage & Retention

🌍 Regional Data Residency

FlockIQ prioritizes local data centers within Nigeria to minimize latency and comply with local data residency requirements. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 standards.

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

Active Accounts

Continuous access to historical batch performance, mortality trends, and financial data for multi-cycle analysis.

Closed Accounts

Data is permanently purged after 90 days following account termination unless otherwise requested for regulatory compliance.

03.

Protection Practices

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

All transmission of farm data from your device to our cloud infrastructure is secured via TLS 1.3 encryption.

📋

Bi-Annual Audits

External security firms perform rigorous penetration tests and compliance audits twice per year.

👥

Access Control (RBAC)

Role-Based Access Control ensures that only authorized farm workers can access specific dashboards, with every action logged.

📤

Data Portability

Export your farm data in CSV or JSON format at any time. Your insights belong to you.

Questions about your flock's data?

Our dedicated Data Protection Officer (DPO) is available to discuss our privacy practices and your rights.
