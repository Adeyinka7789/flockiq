Anomaly Detection Limits

Our anomaly detection algorithms monitor behavioral and physiological data points. While highly accurate in identifying statistical outliers, they may:

  • Produce false positives during environmental shifts.
  • Miss non-symptomatic or rapid-onset conditions.
  • Be influenced by sensor hardware calibration errors.

Always verify system alerts with physical inspection and professional consultation.
