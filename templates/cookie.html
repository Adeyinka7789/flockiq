Cookie Policy

FlockIQ uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze performance, and secure our platform. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices.

Last updated: May 20, 2026
01.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

FlockIQ uses both "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device until you delete them) and "session cookies" (which are deleted when you close your browser).

02.

How FlockIQ Uses Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

🔐 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the platform to function. They enable you to log in, navigate between pages, and access secure areas. Without these cookies, our services cannot be provided.

📊 Performance & Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with FlockIQ by collecting anonymous data about page views, time on site, and user flows. This helps us improve the platform's performance and user experience.

🎨 Functional Cookies

These remember your preferences — such as language selection, dashboard layout, and notification settings — to provide a personalized experience.

04.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These include:

  • Google Analytics — Helps us understand how users engage with FlockIQ
  • Termii — Powers our SMS notification system
  • OpenWeather — Provides weather intelligence for your farm location
  • PayStack — Handles secure payment processing

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or tracking networks.

05.

Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit FlockIQ, you will see a cookie banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can also:

  • Change your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Use your browser's "incognito" or "private browsing" mode
  • Opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

🍪 We use cookies to enhance your experience. By continuing to use FlockIQ, you accept our use of cookies.
